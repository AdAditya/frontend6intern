Rojgari Services Private Limited, registered under Company Act in 2012, is a national agency working to empower job seekers with a wide range of employment related information and service, particularly jobs counselling, soft skills training, and employment match-making. Rojgari enlists and connects job seekers and employers in Nepal through an online portal www.rojgari.com and ‘Employment Shop’, a brick and mortar physical outlet branded as 'Rojgari Pasal'. Currently, the main service outlet of Rojgari Pasal is in Kathmandu (New Baneshwor) -Province 3 with branches in Pokhara - Province 4, Itahari - Province 1, Birgunj - Province 2 and Nepalgunj - Province 5 along with disable-friendly 'Ghumti Rojgari Pasal, a mobile employment shop equipped with staff, equipment, and systems, that travels to different villages, cities, and neighbourhoods with high concentration of job seekers, particularly those that belong to excluded, disadvantaged groups and poorer people living in the rural areas.

Job Description

Conduct cage transactions occurring during the shift which include the following:

  • Issue and receive table fills and credits
  • Accepting patron’s deposits into deposit accounts
  • Receive chips, tokens, cash equivalents and markers in exchange of cash
  • Handle cash advance transactions
  • Prepare closeout reports
  • Check currency, coins and chips in the cashier’s float at the start of the shift. Bills should always be piece-counted to ensure that the amount stated in the Open/Close (Cage Float) is correct
  • Summarize all transactions made during assigned shift and request the supervisor for replenishment of same
  • Balance float at the end of shift
  • When working in the credit administration area, participate in the daily processing of patron credit and check cashing applications and assume responsibility for maintenance of subsidiary records and accumulation of data required for reports
  • Perform duties in other areas of the cash desk that have been requested by the cash desk manager
  • Perform day-to-day administrative tasks such as maintaining files and processing paperwork
